Since 2010

Rooted in Tradition, Crafted for Today

Alshafi Herbal Products began as a small family practice rooted in classical Unani herbal wisdom, founded on the belief that nature holds the key to lasting wellness. What started as a modest herbal counter has grown into a trusted name for thousands of families across Pakistan.

Every Majoon, Kushta, oil, and syrup we prepare follows formulations refined over generations — tested not in a marketing lab, but in the daily lives of the people who trust us with their family's health.
